The Weekend

Our wedding will take place on Sunday 23rd of August. We have our venue, Knockerdown Cottages, from the evening of Friday the 21st until the morning of Monday the 24th of August. We’d love to invite you to celebrate with us throughout the weekend. You’re welcome to join from Friday evening or on Saturday if you prefer.

Please note, this weekend is not the bank holiday in August.

Timeline

  • Friday: Arrive from 6pm, Shabbat dinner
  • Saturday: Games & activities, Dinner
  • Sunday: Ceremony, Speeches, Dancing, Food
  • Monday: Breakfast, Checkout by 10am

About Knockerdown

We’re lucky to have exclusive use of Knockerdown holiday village from Friday evening until Monday morning. There are 16 cottages on site where most of our guests can stay, or you can stay nearby and join us at the site during the days.

The site includes an indoor swimming pool, pool and table tennis tables, a children’s play area, and lots of lovely space to relax. There is also a reservoir nearby and lots of scenic walks in the area. You can see the site plan here.

For those staying on site, you will stay in one of the 16 cottages at Knockerdown. These will be shared between groups of friends and family who know each other and will be allocated nearer the time. All of the cottages have full kitchens so you can self-cater throughout the weekend. You can see more about each cottage here.
